## Read of the category right now

This is a solid first dairy pass:
- greek yogurt lands as the clear elite dairy anchor
- cheddar now sits in the intended middle band instead of sneaking into S
- ice cream stays a clean D outcome

## What the ruleset is currently saying

- dairy is now meaningfully sensitive to harsh saturated-fat and cholesterol profiles
- protein and calcium still matter a lot, but they no longer overpower major fat-side downsides
- sugary weak-protein dairy foods get punished hard, which is exactly what we wanted

## Remaining concerns

- cheddar still has huge raw strengths, so whether it should be high B or low A may depend on later channel philosophy
- greek yogurt looks right, but broader anchors like cottage cheese, milk, skyr, and kefir would help stabilize the category
- minerals may still be a bit too calcium-dominated on their own
