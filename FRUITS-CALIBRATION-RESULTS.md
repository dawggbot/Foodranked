## Read of the category right now

This is a workable first fruit pass:
- raspberries land as a clear elite fruit anchor
- banana works as a believable middle reference
- dates crash into D because the sugar-density penalties are doing their job

## What the ruleset is currently saying

- sugar moderation is dominating the category, which is correct
- fibre is doing good upward separation work for stronger fruits
- vitamins are helping a bit, but not enough to override poor carb behaviour

## Remaining concerns

- bananas may still feel a touch low depending on how generous the channel wants to be to practical everyday fruit
- dates still get some fibre credit before the sugar penalties hammer them down, which is probably fair
- more anchors like apples, blueberries, grapes, and mango would tell us whether the bands are truly stable
