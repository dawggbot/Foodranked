## Read of the category right now

This is a strong first legumes pass:
- lentils land as the clear elite category fit
- chickpeas work as a believable middle reference
- baked beans crash properly into D as a compromised processed legume outcome

## What the ruleset is currently saying

- low GI and fibre are doing exactly the kind of separation we want
- protein helps strong legumes, but does not rescue weak sugary legumes
- fats are staying low-impact, which is correct for this category

## Remaining concerns

- vitamins are effectively irrelevant in this first pass, which may be acceptable
- chickpeas may be slightly flattering on fats relative to how little fat should matter here
- more anchors like black beans, kidney beans, split peas, and soybeans will tell us whether the current bands are truly stable
