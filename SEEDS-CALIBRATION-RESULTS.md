## Read of the category right now

This is a strong first seed pass:
- chia lands as the clear elite seed anchor
- sesame works as a believable middle reference
- roasted salted watermelon seeds land as a definite D anchor

## What the ruleset is currently saying

- fibre is doing real separation work, which is good for seeds
- omega-3 is heavily rewarding true seed monsters like chia
- minerals are correctly helping sesame a lot
- protein still helps weak seed foods more than ideal, but not enough to stop the D anchor from landing in D

## Remaining concerns

- vitamins are basically irrelevant in this category right now, which may be fine
- watermelon seeds still look a bit too respectable on protein and minerals before context penalties hit
- more anchors like flax, hemp, pumpkin, and sunflower will tell us whether the bands are really stable
