# SEEDS-CALIBRATION

## Target anchors
- Chia Seeds → S
- Sesame Seeds → B
- Watermelon Seeds (Roasted & Salted) → D

## Current calibration strategy
- create a seed-specific ruleset from scratch
- anchor the category around one definite S, one definite D, and one middle reference
- push seeds harder toward fibre + omega-3 + mineral dominance than nuts

## Why these anchors
- **Chia seeds** are the clearest elite seed-category fit: absurd omega-3, absurd fibre, strong minerals
- **Sesame seeds** are a strong middle reference: elite minerals, good fats, solid fibre, but not a total monster across every lane
- **Watermelon seeds (roasted & salted)** make a useful definite D anchor because the seed-category penalties are obvious: weak fibre density, rougher fat profile, and a major salt-format downside

## First-pass seed philosophy
- fats should matter a lot
- minerals should matter a lot
- fibre should be one of the biggest separators in the entire category
- protein matters, but should not rescue weak seed-category foods too much
- vitamins are low-priority here

## Active tier targets
This first pass aims for:
- Chia Seeds → S
- Sesame Seeds → B or A/B edge
- Watermelon Seeds (Roasted & Salted) → D

If the ordering is wrong, change weights or bands.
If the ordering is right but tiers are compressed, change thresholds.
