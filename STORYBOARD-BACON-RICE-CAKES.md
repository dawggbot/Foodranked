# BACON — STORYBOARD + MOTION BEATS

## Editorial intent
Bacon should feel:
1. instantly appealing
2. briefly defended
3. then clearly defeated by its own category drawbacks

The emotional rhythm is:
- temptation
- maybe-it's-not-that-bad
- no, actually, it is D tier

## Runtime target
- current build basis: ~79s estimated
- if voiced faster later, keep visual pacing the same and compress only dead gaps

## Scene 1 — Hook
### Time
0.00–2.40

### Narration
`Bacon ranked.`

### Visual
- blurred smoky-red background
- glossy bacon stack hero centered in main stage
- header already populated: Bacon / meat / per 100g / kcal
- title text `BACON`
- second hit: `RANKED`

### Motion beats
- **0.00** background fade in from dark maroon
- **0.10** hero bacon pops in at 96% scale and eases to 100%
- **0.45** `BACON` appears with crisp pixel-slide up
- **1.00** `RANKED` drops in with heavier impact sound
- **1.30–2.40** tiny steam/sizzle loop starts

### Audio cues
- low arcade hit on title
- heavier drop on `RANKED`
- soft sizzling loop under hook only

### Subtitle placement
- one centered line near lower third: `Bacon ranked.`

## Scene 2 — Fats
### Time
2.40–10.65

### Narration
`fat is 42g. saturated fat is a real drawback here. for meats, fat quality is one of the main tie-breakers after protein.`

### Visual
- title chip: `FATS`
- macro headline: `42g`
- submetric cards:
  - `saturated fat 14g`
  - `polyunsaturated fat 4.5g`
- saturated-fat card tinted warning red-purple

### Motion beats
- **2.40** section chip slides in from left
- **2.70** `42g fat` macro flips on
- **3.50** saturated-fat card hits first with short warning pulse
- **5.10** polyunsaturated card appears second, calmer treatment
- **6.30** subtle tie-breaker connector line glows between fat card and category chip
- **8.20–10.65** hold stable for subtitle readability

### UI direction
- make saturated fat the dominant visual weight
- do not clutter with extra micro labels
- keep empty third slot hidden or ghosted, not visible as a blank box

### Subtitle behavior
Break into 2 subtitle chunks:
1. `Fat is 42g. Saturated fat is a real drawback here.`
2. `For meats, fat quality is a main tie-breaker after protein.`

# Shared subtitle / motion notes for both prototypes

## Subtitle density
- keep per-clause reveal rhythm tight
- never let more than one new bullet and one new subtitle chunk compete at once
- in pros/cons scenes, subtitle box should sit above bullet block top edge

## Frame safety
- header remains fixed throughout
- progress dots stay visible at footer
- avoid showing empty placeholder slots when a section has only 1-2 useful metrics

## Build notes
These two episodes should be built first because together they test:
- strong negative verdict pacing
- appetizing-open / negative-close emotional arc
- practical pros vs nutritional cons balance
- whether subtitle placement survives long pros/cons lines
