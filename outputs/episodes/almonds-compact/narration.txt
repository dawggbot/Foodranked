pros first: fairly satiating for the portion because dense texture and fibre can help fullness. easy to use as a practical wholefood snack because convenient and shelf-stable enough to fit many routines. contains useful polyphenols because adds plant-compound value beyond the standard nutrient table.

the drawbacks next: contains some anti-nutrients because phytates may reduce mineral absorption somewhat. easy to overeat calories quickly because energy density can add up fast in large handfuls. not suitable for everyone because tree nut allergy risk matters in practice.

Almonds does have some solid upside. but the tradeoffs keep it from climbing higher. best in small amounts where the fats and minerals matter more than sheer calories.
