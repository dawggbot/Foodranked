the upsides first: practical staple carb source because tubers are often judged by how usable they are day to day. whole-food carb package because that matters because it is a cleaner baseline than heavily refined carbs. can be quite filling because a useful practical strength when the preparation stays sensible.

now the downsides: still a carb-heavy category because that is the core ceiling tubers have to manage. preparation changes outcomes a lot because what you do to a tuber can make or break it. protein support is weak because tubers still need help from the rest of the meal.

Beetroot does have a few real-world upsides. but the nutrition case is still weak and the downsides drag it down hard. best only when you want easy carbs and can accept the missing upside.
