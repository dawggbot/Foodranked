Coffee ranked.

simple one.

0g of carbs. and that is basically the whole nutrition story.

no real protein story here.

no real vitamin story here.

no real mineral story here.

the upsides first: strong misc-category fit because coffee is still fundamentally judged by effects and use-case rather than nutrient composition. strong alertness effect because this is the main real-world reason coffee matters in this category. usually near-zero calories plain because very clean base use-case when not loaded with extras.

on the weaker side: caffeine tradeoffs are real because sleep disruption, jitters, and overuse risk stop this from being a clean automatic S. habit dependence can creep in because a stronger practical downside than green tea usually carries. people often ruin it with sugary add-ins because the practical version consumed is not always the clean black-coffee version.

Coffee does have some solid upside. but the tradeoffs keep it from climbing higher. best treated as a context item rather than a nutritional cornerstone.

B tier.
