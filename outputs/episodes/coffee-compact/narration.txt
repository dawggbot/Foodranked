The upsides first: strong misc-category fit. Coffee is still fundamentally judged by effects and use-case rather than nutrient composition. Strong alertness effect. This is the main real-world reason coffee matters in this category. Usually near-zero calories plain. Very clean base use-case when not loaded with extras.

-

On the weaker side: caffeine tradeoffs are real. Sleep disruption, jitters, and overuse risk stop this from being a clean automatic S. Habit dependence can creep in. A stronger practical downside than green tea usually carries. People often ruin it with sugary add-ins. The practical version consumed is not always the clean black-coffee version.

-

Coffee does have some solid upside. But the tradeoffs keep it from climbing higher. Best treated as a context item rather than a nutritional cornerstone.
