Flax Seeds!

-

Ranked!

-

42.2g of fat. Omega 3 is doing a lot of the work here. For seeds, the fat profile is one of the biggest reasons they earn their place.

-

28.9g of carbs. Fibre is doing a lot of the work here. For a seed, density is expected, so the quality has to show up too.

-

18.3g of protein. Essential amino acid support is one of the better parts here. Protein is a bonus, but not enough by itself.

-

The rest of the section is nothing special.

-

Magnesium at 95% daily value, zinc at 44% daily value, iron at 32% daily value stand out most. That gives this food real mineral support.

-

Pros first: absurd omega-3 density. This should threaten the top of the seeds category. Extreme fibre density. Another elite seed trait. Strong mineral support. Very hard to dismiss nutritionally.

-

Now the downsides: best used ground/processed appropriately. Whole-seed use can reduce payoff. Not universally loved. Practical downside. Can be rough for some. Very high fibre is not free for everyone.

-

Flax Seeds has a genuinely strong overall case. The practical downsides are real, but they do not outweigh the upside. Best as a support food that boosts meals instead of carrying them alone.

-

A tier.
