on the weaker side. micronutrient payoff is weak because that is a major problem in a category meant to reward nutrient density. fibre payoff is weak for a vegetable because it does not offer much of the maintenance-and-repair value the category is supposed to reward. mostly water with limited nutritional return because very low payoff compared with stronger vegetables.
