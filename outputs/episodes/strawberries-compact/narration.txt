-

Minerals are basically not part of the case here.

-

The upsides first: sweetness is kept unusually well in check. That makes the fruit profile much cleaner than average. Usually beats ultra-processed sweet snacks. That replacement value matters in real life. Ingredient simplicity is hard to argue with. Whole-fruit clarity is part of the category appeal.

-

The drawbacks next: protein contribution is tiny. That is normal for fruit, but it still limits nutritional breadth. Can be easy to waste if timing is off. Perishability is a practical downside people actually feel. Satiety is not especially strong. Fruit often needs help from the rest of the meal.

-

Strawberries has a genuinely strong overall case. The practical downsides are real, but they do not outweigh the upside. Best when you want sweetness that still earns its place nutritionally.
